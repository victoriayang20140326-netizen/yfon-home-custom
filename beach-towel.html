What Is A Custom Beach Towel

Quick-drying and ultra-absorbent, our custom beach towels are perfect for brands, resorts, and retailers who want a light, high-performance product that makes waves on the sand. Made from premium microfiber fabric, they dry 3x faster than cotton, pack flat for travel, and feel incredibly soft against skin.

Available in countless prints and patterns that are sure to look stunning on the beach, poolside, or in a resort room. We support full-color reactive printing for sharp, vibrant designs that maintain their brightness through hundreds of washes.

From branded hotel towels and resort merchandise to e-commerce product lines and promotional gifts — our beach towels are fully customizable in size, weight, print, and packaging. A compact, lightweight product with high perceived value and universal appeal.
