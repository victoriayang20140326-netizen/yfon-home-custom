What Are Custom Soft Enamel Pins

Also known as Embossed Pins, custom soft enamel lapel pins offer the same full-color selections as hard enamel pins — but with a distinctive difference. The metal lines are slightly more raised, and the enamel colors sit in recessed areas, giving the pins an interesting appearance of layering and depth that catches the eye.

This textured, layered look makes soft enamel pins a popular choice for brands, organizations, schools, and collectors. They are more economy-friendly than hard enamel while still delivering vibrant, high-detail results — making them ideal for large promotional runs, fan merchandise, and corporate gifting.

Best uses: custom soft enamel pins are perfect for identifying and promoting schools, corporations, military units, associations, sports teams, charities, and artist collectives. Available in any shape, size, and plating finish to match your brand aesthetic.
