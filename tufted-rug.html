What is a Custom Tufted Rug?

In hotels, retail stores, or home interiors, plush tufted carpets are a common sight. Through the tufting process, yarn is vertically embedded into a backing fabric to create a dense pile surface. Their soft texture and diverse designs have made them a popular choice.

Custom tufted carpets represent an upgrade from standard versions: the former are mass-produced items with fixed dimensions and universal patterns, while the latter can be tailored to specific needs in terms of size, color palette, and design –even incorporating exclusive elements like brand logos.

Tufting achieves the perfect balance between "high-efficiency production" and "design flexibility" –producing intricate patterns up to 5–0x faster than hand-knotting, while delivering a fuller pile texture than standard machine-woven carpets.
