What is a Custom Tufted Rug?

In hotels, retail stores, or home interiors, plush tufted carpets are a common sight. Through the tufting process, yarn is vertically embedded into a backing fabric to create a dense pile surface. Their soft texture and diverse design possibilities make them a favorite for custom decor.

Custom tufted carpets represent an upgrade from standard versions: the former are mass-produced items with fixed dimensions and universal patterns, while the latter can be tailored to specific needs in terms of size, color, and shape.

Tufting achieves the perfect balance between "high-efficiency production" and "design flexibility" - producing intricate patterns up to 5-10x faster than hand-knotting, while delivering a premium, thick feel.
