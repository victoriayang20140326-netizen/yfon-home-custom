What Is A Custom Tufted Rug

At YFON HOME, we specialize in crafting and delivering high-quality, custom carpets and rugs for bulk orders and wholesale supply. With years of expertise in the carpet manufacturing industry, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to offer luxurious, durable, and bespoke flooring solutions.

Custom tufted carpets represent an upgrade from standard versions: the former are mass-produced items with fixed dimensions and universal patterns, while the latter can be tailored to specific needs in terms of size, color palette, and design — even incorporating exclusive elements like brand logos.

Tufting achieves the perfect balance between "high-efficiency production" and "design flexibility," producing intricate patterns up to 5-10x faster than hand-knotting, while delivering a fuller pile texture than standard machine-woven carpets.
