What Is A Custom Die-Cut Pillow

From pets and people to cars, trophies, and unforgettable moments — we transform your favorite photo or artwork into a custom-shaped, ultra-soft pillow you can actually hug. Each pillow is precision die-cut to match the exact silhouette of your image, printed in vibrant full color on both sides, and handmade with care.

Unlike standard square pillows, die-cut pillows follow the contour of your design — whether it's a character shape, brand mascot, logo, or any irregular form. This makes them ideal for brand merchandise, fan collections, promotional gifts, and retail product lines.

Whether it's a heartfelt gift or a high-impact branded product, our die-cut pillows combine comfort with personality. Premium PP cotton fill gives a plush, soft feel that lasts, while the hidden zipper allows easy refilling or washing.
