What is a Custom Woven Blanket?

Wrap yourself in the warmth and comfort of our best-selling, heirloom quality woven throw blankets, woven to order from 100% cotton or polyester cotton mixed in a range of beautiful designs.

These versatile pieces add a touch of style and texture to any room, whether draped over your couch or laid across your bed. Unlike printed blankets, the design is woven directly into the fabric using the jacquard technique, creating a rich, artistic texture.

Why Choose Our Custom Woven Blankets?

1
Heirloom Quality - Durable and timeless woven construction that lasts for years.
2
Artistic Texture - Intricate jacquard weaving adds depth and a premium feel.
3
Cotton Tassels - Classic fringe finish for a traditional and stylish look.
4
Versatile Use - Perfect as a throw, wall tapestry, or cozy wrap.
