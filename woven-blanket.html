What Is A Custom Woven Blanket

Wrap yourself in the warmth and comfort of our best-selling, heirloom-quality woven throw blankets. Woven to order from a 35% cotton and 65% polyester blend, our jacquard woven blankets offer a rich texture and long-lasting durability that printed alternatives simply cannot match.

Jacquard weaving embeds patterns directly into the fabric structure — not printed on top. This means the design is integral to the textile itself: sharper edges, more dimensional texture, and colors that never fade or peel. Available in double-sided and single-sided constructions, with authentic 100% cotton tassels for a premium finishing touch.

These versatile pieces add style and texture to any room — draped over a couch, laid across a bed, or presented as a premium brand gift. Ideal for luxury retailers, hotels, interior designers, and brands seeking high-perceived-value textile products.
