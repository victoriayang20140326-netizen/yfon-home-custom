What Is A Custom Fleece Blanket

Each custom fleece blanket is soft, warm, and durable. Made from premium cozy fleece fabric that is thick, high-density, and free from shedding and fading. Browse our range of comforters, sherpa fleece, and microfiber options to create the perfect blanket for any season or occasion.

Fleece blankets are among the most popular custom textile products — lightweight, breathable, and exceptionally soft against skin. They machine wash easily and retain their shape and color through hundreds of cycles, making them a reliable choice for retail, hotels, corporate gifting, and branded merchandise.

From a simple logo print to a full photographic design, we handle any level of customization. Our production team ensures precise color matching and consistent quality across all order sizes — from 1 piece to 10,000+.
