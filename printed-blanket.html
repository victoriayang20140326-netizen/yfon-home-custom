What Is A Custom Printed Blanket

Each custom printed blanket is soft, warm, and durable. Using advanced sublimation printing technology, we reproduce your artwork, logo, or photo in vibrant full color that won't fade or peel — even after repeated machine washing.

Printed blankets are made from 100% premium polyester, offering excellent softness, warmth, and color retention. They're lightweight, breathable, and available in any size — making them the ideal choice for branded merchandise, promotional gifts, retail product lines, and seasonal campaigns.

Whether you're a brand launching a merch line, a retailer sourcing custom holiday gifts, or a hotel equipping guestrooms — our printed blankets deliver consistent quality at scale, with flexible order quantities starting from just 1 piece.
